About Me

About Me

My name is Makgabo Mathato and I'm based in Clayville, Gauteng. I am married and I stay with my family.

My experience:I have been working in the telecomunication industry for 11 years now as an inventory controller. The technology I was using on a daily basis includes: Oracle, SCADA, WMS, RDT... which were mainly for the transactions of stock (handsets) and other electronics. Late 2019 I started to develop an interest in Technology then I started with some online courses on Udemy in Full Stack Web Development, after which I enrolled at HyperionDev.com in June 2020 after realising programming is really my passion.
